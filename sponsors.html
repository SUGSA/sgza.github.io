When you sponsor a Regional Scrum Gathering®, your company’s message is exactly where it should be – in front of your target audience. The Regional Scrum Gathering® South Africa attracts around 200or more attendees every year and has a track record of delivering world-class content. The attendees are made up of scrum practitioners, scrum masters, product owners, developers, management, executives, agile coaches, trainers and many other roles. This means your message is viewed and captured by scrum and agile community members from every level of the organisation.

Regional Scrum Gathering® South Africa 2019 will be our 10th edition of the conference. The event attracts top level speakers internationally and locally. Every year it provides great opportunities to promote your products and services as well as to find the right talent for your company.
